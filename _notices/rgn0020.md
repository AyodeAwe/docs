## Overview

`cuDF` was hotfixed to version `21.12.01`.

## Rationale

`cudf` was hotfixed to `21.12.01` in order to fix the bug, [#9868](https://github.com/rapidsai/cudf/issues/9868).

## Impact

All v21.12 users are encouraged to update the `cuDF` library to `21.12.01` as soon as possible.
