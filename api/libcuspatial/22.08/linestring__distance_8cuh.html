The shortest distance between two linestrings is defined as the shortest distance between all pairs of segments of the two linestrings. If any of the segments intersect, the distance is 0.

Template Parameters
Cart2dItAiterator type for point array of the first linestring of each pair. Must meet the requirements of LegacyRandomAccessIterator and be device-accessible.
Cart2dItBiterator type for point array of the second linestring of each pair. Must meet the requirements of LegacyRandomAccessIterator and be device-accessible.
OffsetIteratoriterator type for offset array. Must meet the requirements of LegacyRandomAccessIterator and be device-accessible.
OutputItiterator type for output array. Must meet the requirements of LegacyRandomAccessIterator and be device-accessible.
